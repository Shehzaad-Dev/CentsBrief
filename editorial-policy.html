Fact-Checking & Editorial Policy

Last Updated: April 14, 2026

1. Our Mission

CentsBrief aims to provide highly accessible, data-driven financial insights for retail audiences in the US and UK. We focus on "information gain"—explaining the why behind market movements to help readers understand the impact on their personal finances.

2. Automated Journalism & AI Disclosure

CentsBrief utilizes a programmatic delivery model. Our news briefs are generated using advanced Large Language Models (LLMs) and custom algorithms that aggregate data from reputable financial RSS feeds (such as Yahoo Finance). This allows us to deliver high-speed market context without human bias or delay.

3. Fact-Checking Standard

Accuracy is our priority. Our system performs a triple-check verification process:

  • Source Validation: We only ingest data from verified institutional financial feeds.
  • Cross-Referencing: Our algorithms cross-reference headlines to ensure consistent reporting across multiple market segments.
  • Integrity Filters: Every brief is screened for sensationalism, financial advice promises, and factual hallucinations before publication.

4. Editorial Independence

CentsBrief is independently owned and operated. We do not accept payment from companies to feature their stocks or financial products. Our coverage is dictated solely by market data and retail relevance.

5. Not Financial Advice

Information provided by CentsBrief is for educational and informational purposes only. It should not be construed as investment, tax, or legal advice. Every financial decision involves risk, and readers should consult with a licensed professional before taking action.
