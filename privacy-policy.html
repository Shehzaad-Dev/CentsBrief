Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how CentsBrief.online processes limited data to operate an automated finance publication for US and UK audiences. We are committed to transparency, proportional data usage, and practical user control. Because our platform does not require account registration, we intentionally reduce personal-data collection wherever possible.

No-Login Architecture

CentsBrief.online does not run a user-login system for reading content. As a result, we do not maintain account profiles, passwords, account dashboards, or subscriber identity records tied to login credentials. This architecture lowers exposure to credential theft and reduces personal data footprint by default.

Data We May Process

To maintain site reliability, analytics quality, and ad operation integrity, we may process technical usage signals such as browser type, device category, approximate region, session behavior, referral source, page performance telemetry, and ad interaction metrics. These signals are generally processed at aggregate or pseudonymous level where feasible.

Cookies and Ad Technology

We and our ad partners may use cookies, local storage tokens, and similar technologies to deliver and measure advertising, control frequency, and support anti-fraud checks. Depending on jurisdiction and consent configuration, non-essential cookie categories may be restricted until user preference is collected.

Legal Frameworks and Rights

Where applicable, CentsBrief aligns with core principles in GDPR, UK-GDPR, and CCPA/CPRA. Users may request access, correction, deletion, or objection regarding personal data processing that can be reasonably linked to their request context. California residents may request data-category disclosures and deletion rights under applicable law.

Data Retention and Security

We retain data only as long as needed for operational, security, legal, and measurement purposes. Retention windows depend on category and legal obligation. We use practical safeguards such as minimized data collection, controlled access, and routine provider-level security practices to reduce misuse risk.

International Access

Because CentsBrief is available online, data may be processed by infrastructure providers in multiple regions. We evaluate vendor controls and apply contractual safeguards where appropriate to support lawful transfers and responsible processing.

Contact and Requests

To submit privacy requests, email centsbreif@gmail.com with subject line "Privacy Request". Include enough context for us to identify affected records. We may request verification details before fulfilling certain rights requests.
