About Us

AI-Powered Market Intelligence for Everyday Decision-Makers

CentsBrief.online is an automated financial information platform built for readers in the United States and United Kingdom who want fast, structured, and transparent market context. Our system uses advanced AI workflows to monitor major macro and market signals, including US Treasury yields, stock market momentum, inflation expectations, and currency-linked risk sentiment.

Our editorial mission is centered on Financial Transparency and Concise Market Education. Instead of publishing noise, we prioritize information gain: what changed, why it matters, and how that shift may affect real-world financial decisions such as saving, borrowing, budgeting, and long-term planning.

We do not sell financial products, brokerage services, insurance, lending products, or managed portfolios. CentsBrief.online is an information service only. We aim to help readers interpret market developments with clarity and consistency while encouraging independent verification through primary sources.

Because our platform is automated, we continuously improve summary quality, source selection, and contextual framing to keep output relevant, balanced, and understandable for non-specialists while still useful for experienced market followers.
