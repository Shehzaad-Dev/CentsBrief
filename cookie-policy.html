Cookie Policy

This Cookie Policy describes how CentsBrief.online and selected technology partners use cookies and related identifiers when you browse our site. Cookies help us keep the platform fast, stable, measurable, and monetizable without requiring account registration.

What Cookies Are

Cookies are small text files stored by your browser. They can remember preference choices, support session continuity, and help measure interactions. Similar technologies include local storage, pixel tags, SDK identifiers, and server-side session tokens.

Cookie Categories

Essential cookies support page rendering, security checks, and consent state management. Analytics cookies help us understand traffic patterns, performance bottlenecks, and content engagement quality. Advertising cookies support ad delivery, campaign reporting, frequency caps, and anti-fraud safeguards.

Advertising Partner Cookies

Ad networks and exchanges, including providers similar to RevBid and Google AdSense, may place cookies to optimize ad relevance, measure impressions, reduce duplicate exposures, and detect invalid traffic. These partners may operate under their own policies and retention schedules.

Consent and Regional Controls

In regions with consent requirements, non-essential cookies may be restricted until consent is captured. You can also manage cookie behavior in browser settings by clearing stored data, blocking third-party cookies, or limiting cross-site tracking. Note that disabling essential categories can affect functionality.

Retention and Security

Cookie durations vary by purpose and provider. Some expire at session end, while others persist for reporting windows. We regularly review implementation scope to reduce unnecessary tracking and align usage with operational necessity and legal obligations.

How to Contact Us

For cookie-related questions, email support@centsbreif.online with subject line "Cookie Policy Request".
